Pizza Hut is an American restaurant chain and international franchise founded in 1958 in Wichita, Kansas, by Dan and Frank Carney. The company is known for its Italian-American cuisine, including pizza and pasta, as well as side dishes and desserts. Pizza Hut has over 18,000 restaurants worldwide, making it one of the largest pizza chains in the world.

At Pizza Hut, we are committed to serving delicious food made with high-quality ingredients. Our menu features a variety of pizzas, including classic favorites and innovative creations, as well as appetizers, salads, pasta dishes, and desserts. Whether you're dining in, ordering delivery, or picking up carryout, Pizza Hut offers something for everyone.

Our mission is to provide our customers with great-tasting food and exceptional service in a welcoming and friendly environment. We strive to innovate and evolve while staying true to our roots, ensuring that every pizza we serve is a slice of perfection.
